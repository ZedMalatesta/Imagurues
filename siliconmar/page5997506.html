ENTREPRENEURSHIP CAMP
Welcome to Silicon Mar. Your Journey Begins Now
Silicon Mar concept is based on our beliefs that Silicon Valley is the n1 global spot for entrepreneurs, innovators and change makers. We will share with you Silicon Valley's change making and risk taking mentality. In the same time unlike Silicon Valley while challenging yourself, working hard and learning new skills you will be able to enjoy many simple pleasures along the way from beachside sunsets to local tapas in a community of likeminded people.
Entrepreneurship mindset, openness to new learning and experiences in a great company of international mentors and likeminded entrepreneurial souls. All this, surrounded by fantastic nature and the endless beaches of Costa Blanca, makes Silicon Mar one of the greatest life changing experiences you can afford and enjoy.

For this unique bootcamp, we're hosting 10 people from creative and entrepreneurial backgrounds to live, work from, and explore Alicante neighborhood together for 2 weeks at a time.
It's about taking an intentional break from your routine, finding new perspective, building new relationships, growing new ideas, and exploring with intention among an incredible backdrop of people and place. We provide housing, workspace, and an unforgettable experience filled with workshops, happy hours, and excursions.
WHAT WILL YOU GET
For all those who are looking for new experiences about exploring the best version of yourself, and living with intention and purpose with an incredible community of peerswhile still being productive. Silicon Mar experience is about you
