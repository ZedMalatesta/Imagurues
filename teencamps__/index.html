TEENGURU CAMP

Easter week: 15 - 17 april 2019
for teenagers aged 10 to 14

Certificate provided in:
technology | entrepreneurship | social skills

Registration

We dedicate each day of our camp to what we believe is crucial for tomorrow of our kids: entrepreneurship, technology and soft skills. Do you want your kid to think independently, and take decisions, understand the influence of technology or become an entrepreneur? Do you want him to learn about business operations and communicate effectively with peers, colleagues or investors? Then welcome to TeenGuru Camp! We have 4+ years of experience in teaching entrepreneurship to kids and teens!

Language of the program: Spanish with several short lections / activities in English (only basic level of English is required for participation).

Entrepreneurship

  • Monday, April 15

  • What is a private company? How it operates?
  • Types of business products and services.
  • How a company communicates with its clients?
  • Importance of sales. Revenue and financial flow.
  • Future finance (cryptocurrencies)

And all this enjoying networking, walking around and visiting corporations.

Technology

  • Tuesday, April 16

  • The concept of internet and ecommerce
  • We talk about blockchain and how we use it in different industries
  • Together in groups we built differents products on Arduino
  • We discuss the importance of technology in our business and personal life

Important part of the day are fun activities!

Soft Skills

  • Wednesday, April 17th

  • Soft skills: why they are so important in our tech times. Soft skills are considered the most important skills for any kind of a job.
  • We will use the most advanced methodology to improve soft skills: Design Thinking methodology from Stanford University.
  • Will discuss the use of social media by teenagers. Teens will learn how social media can help them in their future professional activity.
  • We will talk about security issues in social media.

By the end of this day we invite parents to see what they kids have learned.

About
Imaguru

Imaguru is a coworking space and a space for community of startups and tech talent in Madrid. Our team organises variuos events and educational programs related to edge technology and coding, business and soft skills. Imaguru's developed a specific program for teenagers called TeenGuru and has already educated 300+ teens. Teens working with Imaguru top notch mentors have developed 100+ business ideas and projects. Finalists of the program win Startups Competitions and build their own businesses. Join our Summer Edition of Teen Guru and let leverage your skills and knowledge during lovely season.
