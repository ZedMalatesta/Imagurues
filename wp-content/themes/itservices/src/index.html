Working process

  1. Kickoff meeeting The first step is to discover more about you, your company's vision, and to build a strong relationship. This is where we will create your brand, create a custom strategy, and set project goals.
  2. IdeationWe use design thinking methodology that helps to find new solutions through creative approach, teamwork and orientation to the user. After this stage, we have a unique design concept for your project.
  3. PrototypingAt this stage we create interactive prototype of a product. Prototype helps to easily understand what appeared to be a good idea on paper, but in fact it breaks usability. We follow these moments and offer better solutions.
  4. DesignWhen the target audience is identified and all the problems are solved on the prototype, we proceed to design. We will analyze the products of your competitors and find what users like and don’t like and will use this info to make your product the best on the market.
  5. ProgrammingAt this stage product design revives. Our developers use cutting-edge technologies to release high quality and effective product.
  6. TestingHere review and testing takes place, which ensures the quality of your project.
  7. Release, promotion, supportWe're here when you need us, long after your site goes live
